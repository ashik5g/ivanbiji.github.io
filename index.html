WHO ARE WE ?

Starting out as IEEE NITC SB's event to promote start up ideas, Interface has now grown into the largest summit in the Malabar region exclusive to budding start-ups. Spanning over 3 days, it provides a platform for student entrepreneurs to discuss their ideas and products with the experienced ones. The primary objective of Interface is to find and support the best and most innovative startup ideas from the numerous abstracts and submissions by colleges and incubation cells across the State.
